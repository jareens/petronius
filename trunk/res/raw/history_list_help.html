1 Introduction

This page shows the list of history records. An history record stores the information about when a garment was worn. If you short press a record its properties will be shown.

2 Options menu

These are the options you can select:

2.1 New history record
Opens a form to enter a new history record's data.

3 Context menu

These are the options listed when you long press a history record:

3.1 Edit history record
Opens a form to edit history record's data.
3.2 Delete history record
Click on it to delete the record.
